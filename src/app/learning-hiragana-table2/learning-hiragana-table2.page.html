ten-ten
(which literally means "dot-dot") is a sign composed of
2 tiny strokes
added on kana syllabaries to indicate that the consonant of a syllable should be
pronounced differently
To easily remember how the letter transforms, pronounce the letter by adding a throat sound, e.g. Z is an S with a throat sound added
