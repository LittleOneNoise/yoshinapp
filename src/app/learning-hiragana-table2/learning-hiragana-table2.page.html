A ten-ten (which literally means "dot-dot") is a sign composed of 2 tiny strokes added on kana syllabaries to indicate that the consonant of a syllable should be pronounced differently
To remember easily how the letter is transformed, think about what would be the letter that is close to it and that use the throat to be pronounced, try it yourself for S for exemple, it only uses the mouth but Z sound requires the throat.
